Alignment

Quaker Homepage

Why I Chose This Page

The header image caught my eye immediately with the alignment of the school supplies around the granola box. Continuing down to the different sections is the alignment of the menus and groupings within the images as well.

Great job Quaker Oats!

Contrast

  • Huggies

Huggies Homepage

Why I Chose This Page

The stark contrast of the white lettering on bright red background is an attention grabber! Enven the coloring of the little crawler is a contrast, from the pale skin to the dark features and hair.

The black of the Huggies logo below is yet another contrast on an awesome home page.

Repetition

  • Pampers

Pampers Homepage

Why I Chose This Page

Pampers has a great history of repeating their logo colors throughout their media. I love the stark orange on the call-to-action button and notification button. It definitely grabs your attention. The teal lettering throughout the webpabe and icons also brings a comforting feeling to the page, if you trust their product..and I do.

Awesome marketing, Pampers!
